    14 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Profession: Actress

    Biography: Emma Stone is an American actress whose career began with a part in the television series In Search of the New Partridge Family. She quickly transitioned to film and her breakout role came in the comedy Superbad (2007), which led to a series of successful films, including Zombieland (2009) and Easy A (2010), the latter earning her a Golden Globe nomination for Best Actress.

    Emma Stone's collaboration with director Damien Chazelle in La La Land (2016) marked a high point in her career. Her performance alongside Ryan Gosling as an aspiring actress in Los Angeles earned her widespread critical acclaim and the Academy Award for Best Actress.

    Born: November 6, 1988
    Birthplace: Scottsdale, Arizona, USA
    Age: 35 years old

    Generation: Millennial
