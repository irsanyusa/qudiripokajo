    We all have our own ways of remembering to do things. Some write it down, some set up reminders on their phones. Some can even boast with a sharp memory and never forgetting things. However, for one man, his solution means he will never forget to do his important things, all owing to his unique and extremely practical tattoo.

    The man invented a brilliant way to stay organized.

    A man named Robert visited a tattoo studio in London with a very practical aim. The tattoo artist Luke Ashley was asked to ink a to-do list on the palm of Robert’s hand.

    “To do list for Robert. Practical palm tattoos. Can’t wait to see this in action,” explained Luke in his Instagram video, that’s gained over 500,000 reactions since it was first posted.

    The tattoo solution quickly gained tons of fans.

    “To do or not to do?” may be the only question that netizens are asking themselves after they first saw the extraordinary tattoo. Both admirers and trolls left dozens of comments on the Instagram expressing their thoughts on Robert’s unusual choice of ink. Some were sure it’s a brilliant idea and called Robert a genius, and others weren’t too sure.

    One person thought it was a brilliant idea as if he ever got Alzheimer’s he wouldn’t have many difficulties. Some other user said he’s cracked the code for ADHD. Some person even said, “He literally has his day controlled in the palm of his hand.”

    “That’s actually just impressively useful,” said another admirer.
