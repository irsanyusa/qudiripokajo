    'DWTS' Pros Daniella Karagach, Pasha Pashkov Indulge in Tropical Babymoon

    Daniella Karagach via Instagram

    Reading Time: 2 minutes

    Dancing With The Stars couple Daniella Karagach and Pasha Pashkov recently embarked on their babymoon together. Evidently, the two are making the most out of their vacation before they excitedly welcome their baby girl.

    Daniella Karagach, Pasha Pashkov Revel in Sultry Beach Vacation

    Karagach and Pashkov have clearly been desiring a stress free trip amid the looming arrival of their first born. In her latest Instagram post, Karagach shared a series of photos of her donning a skimpy two-piece bikini with an adorable baby bump. The two were in a beach in Puerto Plata, Dominican Republic for a sponsored trip that served as their babymoon.

    “Thank you Virgin Voyages for making this trip so special. Pash and I wanted to do something different for our baby moon and this couldn’t have been more perfect,” she wrote. “So grateful for this amazing experience.”

    In the same post, Karagach revealed that their babymoon doesn’t end with one stop. Rather, she and Pashkov plan to relax for another vacation in San Juan, Puerto Rico.

    Last December, the two announced that they will be having a baby girl as first-time parents. By May this year, Karagach and Pashkov will welcome their newborn, one which they have been anticipating since they got married.

    The DWTS Cast is Excited to Welcome Their Baby Girl

    In Karagach’s post, some DWTS cast have expressed their excitement for the dancing parents. Pashkov was first to comment, calling her wife his “baby mama.” Meanwhile, Peta Murgatroyd cannot help but point Karagach’s obvious pregnancy bump.

    “baby girl is poppin !!” she wrote.

    Lindsay Arnold is also obviously thrilled about the arrival of Karagach’s baby, greeting her with an enthusiastic “hi.” Selma Blair, on the other hand, wished her a delightful vacation.

    “A wonderful baby moon to you. Gorgeous,” she wrote.

    Heidi D’Amelio, Amanda Kloots, and Cheryl Burke also expressed their happiness over such pregnancy. Meanwhile, aside from the well wishes, Karagach received praises regarding her stunning figure despite being pregnant.
