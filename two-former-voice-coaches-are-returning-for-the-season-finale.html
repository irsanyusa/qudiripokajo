    Ready for The Voice two-part finale this week? Not only will we get to hear performances from the Top 5 and find out who the Season 18 winner is, but we’ll also get to see appearances form some pretty big stars.

    The two-night finale will feature performances from the four coaches, as well as Lady Antebellum, Bon Jovi, Bebe Rexha, and KAROL G. The Voice also just announced that two former coaches will be back to perform!

    Which Coaches Are Back On ‘The Voice’ Finale?

    CeeLo Green and Shakira will reportedly be back on the show Tuesday night for a performance with the current coaches, Top 9 contestants, and former Comeback Stage coach Bebe Rexha. They’ll be singing “Let My Love Open the Door” by Pete Townshend to open the episode.

    SEE ALSO: FULL VIDEO OF J.LO TEACHING SHAKIRA THE BOOTY SHAKE ON SET AT THE SUPER BOWL

    CeeLo was one of the original coaches in Season 1 of The Voice, along with Blake Shelton, Adam Levine, and Christina Aguilera. He left the show after Season 5. Shakira was a coach in Seasons 4 and 6. Earlier this year, she performed during the Super Bowl halftime show with Jennifer Lopez.

    ‘The Voice’ Season 18 Is Coming To A Close

    Season 18 of The Voice will end in a two-night finale this Monday and Tuesday on NBC. On Monday night, starting at 8 pm ET, the Top 5 artists will perform. This season, it’s down to CammWess (Team Legend), Todd Tilghman (Team Blake), Micah Iverson (Team Kelly), Thunderstorm Artis (Team Nick), and Toneisha Harris (Team Blake).

    SEE ALSO: ‘THE VOICE’ RESULTS: WHICH ARTISTS ARE GOING TO THE HISTORIC FINALE?

    The results show will air Tuesday night at 9 pm ET, as the winner is revealed. As with the past several episodes, the coaches and contestants will appear remotely. According to Yahoo, other performances in the finale include a duet between Blake Shelton and Gwen Stefani, and one from Nick Jonas and his brothers.
