    If you want your room to have a pleasant aroma all day long, try applying some perfume on a cool light bulb. Later, when you turn the lights on, the bulb will heat up and disperse the smell throughout the room. We’ll probably never be able to count how many ways to make our routine a little bit more interesting. But we know that the products below will help you do just that.

    1. Take your bathing experience up a level with this stand. Put your phone, a snack, and even candles on it. It slides out to fit any size bathtub.

    Made of bamboo. It has a hinged frame that keeps your book or tablet upright and stable for hands-free reading. It also has openings for a glass, a razor, and other small items.

    2. Now, you will have your own unicorn to help you make tea! Just fill its stainless-steel bottom with your favorite loose-leaf tea, screw it on top, and enjoy.

    The mesh at the bottom of the unicorn will prevent tea leaves from spilling into the mug. This tea infuser is reusable and easy to wash on the top shelf of the dishwasher.

    3. A pinch of fun! Click on the snail’s shell to get soap from its mouth. Both adults and children will enjoy washing their hands.

    It can hold all kinds of hygienic liquids, like shampoo, hand soap, shower gel, conditioner, and even dish detergent. It will hold 120 ml of liquid.

    4. Darth Vader and Baby Yoda will keep an eye on your dishwasher! These magnets will make it more convenient for you to know when there are clean or dirty dishes.

    It comes with one waterproof and double-sided flip magnet. Simply flip it over with the right side up. Don’t worry if your dishwasher is non-magnetic — a sticky iron plate is included.

    5. Turn an ordinary evening into some fun. Make ice cream in dinosaur shapes with the help of these silicone ice molds.

    This set comes with 4 different dinosaur shapes, but many other animal shapes are also available. The handles on the tray make it easy to hold. The silicone molds are also dishwasher-safe.

    6. This bouquet will interest every family member, even children! Build it yourself out of Lego pieces and add a bit of creativity to your space.

    The compilation consists of 756 pieces, so it’ll hold your interest for a long time. It features 15 stems with a mix of flower and leaf varieties like roses, snapdragons, poppies, asters, daisies, and grasses. The flowers have customizable elements: posable petals and leaves, with adjustable stem lengths to create wonderful arrangements for the home.

    7. These cacti will clean bottles or hard-to-reach small places in your home. Each cactus trunk is a different kind of brush.

    The stand has holes for moisture to escape. Brush sets are available in other designs too. Product dimensions: 3.1 cm x 3.1 cm x 27.46 cm.

    8. Give your precious glasses a safe and comfortable respite. This koala will gladly help!

    Perfect for most standard eyeglasses or sunglasses. This eyeglass retainer can also hold small items like rings, necklaces, watches, or hair bands. Size: 15.75 cm x 14.73 cm x 10.92 cm

    9. No one else will ever say that cooking is boring. This silicone frog will make separating the yolks from the whites easier.

    Very easy to use: just squeeze the frog, bring the muzzle to the yolk, let go to collect it. Also available in the shape of a fish.

    10. Turn cleaning into a pleasure! Even your kids will want to mop the floors with these teddy bear microfiber slippers.

    The microfiber sole is held down with Velcro. You can wear the slippers just like you would an ordinary pair. They have soft soles that will not make noise on the floor and are available in different colors and sizes.

    11. Throw out the boring sink nets. This cute duckling will make catching leftovers after cooking or hair in the bathroom more fun!

    Simple to assemble. The strainer fits all standard-sized sink drains to prevent them from clogging. Made of stainless steel and silicone. Also available with flamingo and whale shapes.

    12. This foam maker will help you create an abundant foam in seconds, reducing the consumption of facial cleanser. Simply fill it with facial cleanser and water, and lather up.

    You can even use it to foam your coffee! It is compact, and great for travel or home use. It only weighs 38 g.
