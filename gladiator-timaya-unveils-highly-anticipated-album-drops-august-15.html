    Gladiator: Timaya unveils highly anticipated album drops August 15

    19 May 2024, 00:00

    Known for his energetic and infectious music, Timaya has been a dominant force in the Nigerian music industry, and fans are eagerly anticipating the release of his latest musical offering.

    GLADIATOR promises to showcase Timaya's distinctive musical style and lyrical prowess, providing fans with an exciting and diverse collection of tracks.

    With his unique blend of Afrobeats, Dancehall, and Pop influences, Timaya continues to push boundaries and captivate audiences with his music.

    ADVERTISEMENT

    The album is set to feature a stellar lineup of collaborations and production from some of the industry's top talents, further adding to the anticipation surrounding its release. GLADIATOR is scheduled for release Thursday, August 15, and fans can expect an unforgettable musical experience from one of Nigeria's most dynamic artists.

    GLADIATOR is more than just an album; it is a statement of resilience, strength, and musical prowess. Timaya, known for his unique voice and infectious rhythms, takes listeners on a journey through various themes, from empowerment and success to love and celebration.

    The album features a stellar lineup of collaborations with some of the biggest names in the industry, adding depth and diversity to the project.

    Tracklist

    1. PAYBACK

    Opening the album with a bold statement, PAYBACK is a track that speaks to themes of karma and justice, setting the tone for the rest of the album.

    2. LIVE-STYLE

    LIVE-STYLE is a vibrant anthem that celebrates living life to the fullest. With its catchy hooks and energetic beats, it’s a track that encourages listeners to embrace their unique lifestyles.

    3. SWEET US

    ADVERTISEMENT

    A quintessential Timaya track, SWEET US is a feel-good song that blends Afrobeat rhythms with a message of self-love and positivity.

    4. COMPOSURE

    On COMPOSURE, Timaya delivers a powerful message about maintaining calm and control in the face of adversity. The track’s laid-back vibe is complemented by its introspective lyrics.

    5. TREASURE ft. Alpha P, Olamide & Phyno

    This star-studded collaboration is one of the highlights of GLADIATOR. TREASURE combines the lyrical prowess of Olamide and Phyno with the fresh talent of Alpha P, resulting in a track that is both rich in content and infectious in rhythm.

    ADVERTISEMENT

    6. FREE

    FREE is a liberating track that explores themes of freedom and self-expression. Timaya’s vocals shine on this song, making it a standout moment on the album.

    7. WHININ CRIMINAL ft. Mr Killa

    Featuring the energetic Mr Killa, WHININ CRIMINAL is a dancehall-infused track that will get listeners moving. The song’s infectious beat and catchy lyrics make it a guaranteed party starter.

    ADVERTISEMENT

    8. IN MY HAND ft. Tiwa Savage

    Timaya teams up with Tiwa Savage on IN MY HAND, a soulful track that blends their unique vocal styles. The song explores themes of love and trust, making it a deeply emotional and memorable collaboration.

    9. DEY YOU DEY

    DEY YOU DEY is a motivational track that encourages listeners to stay true to themselves and persevere through challenges. Timaya’s inspiring lyrics are delivered with conviction, making this a powerful anthem.

    10. SMILE

    ADVERTISEMENT

    A feel-good track, SMILE is all about spreading joy and positivity. With its uplifting lyrics and infectious melody, this song is sure to bring a smile to listeners' faces.

    11. BLESSINGS ft. Eugy Official

    BLESSINGS is a gratitude-filled track that features the smooth vocals of Eugy Official. The song is a celebration of life’s blessings, with a laid-back groove that is perfect for reflective moments.

    12. NA MONEY ft. Konshens

    In NA MONEY, Timaya teams up with dancehall star Konshens for a track that celebrates success and financial independence. The song’s catchy chorus and danceable beat make it an instant hit.

    13. GET MY MONEY RIGHT

    Closing the album on a high note, GET MY MONEY RIGHT is an anthem of financial empowerment and success. Timaya’s confident delivery and the track’s energetic beat make it a perfect finale for the GLADIATOR album.
