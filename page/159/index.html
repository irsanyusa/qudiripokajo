The African tribe in Zimbabwe with odd toes

11 Jun 2024, 00:00

This tribe is the only traditional hunter-gatherers indigenous to Zimbabwe. Ectrodactyly: The genetic trait of the Vadoma This genetic condition is characterised by the absence of the three middle toes and the inward turning of the remaining two, creating a foot shape that resembles an ostrich's. This unusual trait is predominant among the Vadoma due to the tribe's long history of isolation and intermarriage within their community.
