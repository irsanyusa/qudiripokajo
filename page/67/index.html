What is the Edinburgh International Festival? The Edinburgh International Festival is the leading event in Scotland's cultural calendar and turns the capital city into the world's cultural hotspot every August. In 2023 Scottish violinist Nicola Benedetti was the first female Festival Director, who has also performed several times at the festival. In 2024, the Edinburgh International Festival will take place from 2 to 25 August celebrating the theme of ‘Rituals That Unite Us’.
