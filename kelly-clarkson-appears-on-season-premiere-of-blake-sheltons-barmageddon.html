    Former The Voice coaches Kelly Clarkson and Blake Shelton recently reunited on the season premiere of Shelton’s show Barmageddon. Clarkson went up against actress Michelle Rodriguez in games like Human Foosball and Air Cannon Cornhole.

    Kelly Clarkson Appears on Blake Shelton’s Barmageddon

    Barmageddon returns for a second season on USA at 11/10c on Monday, November 13. The series was created by Shelton and The Voice host Carson Daly. It features celebrity contestants playing extreme versions of bar games.

    For the Season 2 premiere, Shelton reunited with his former co-star Clarkson. In a clip shared on Instagram, the show’s host Nikki Garcia explains the rules to Human Foosball. In addition to Clarkson and Rodriguez, Shelton and Daly are also part of the game.

    We’re used to seeing Shelton and Clarkson getting competitive as coaches on The Voice, but this is a whole new ballgame – literally! The coaches are on opposing teams in this match, which puts a supersized spin on the classic tabletop game.

    In another preview clip shared by People, Clarkson and Rodriguez can be seen playing a game of cornhole using an air cannon. There’s plenty of innuendo as the celebrities try to help their contestants win.

    SEE ALSO: BLAKE SHELTON’S ‘BARMAGEDDON’ IS RETURNING FOR SEASON 2 THIS NOVEMBER

    What Else Can We Expect This Season?

    Barmageddon is filmed at Shelton’s Nashville bar Ole Red. Earlier this month, Shelton and Daly teased what fans can expect from Season 2 of the show in a preview. Celebrity guests this season include Jelly Roll, Joey Fatone, Chris Hardwick, Carly Pearce, and Boyz II Men.

    Shelton’s wife Gwen Stefani will also make an appearance this season, going up against her husband. Shelton shared that Stefani is “the least competitive person that I have ever met in my life.”

    In the preview, Stefani jokes to Shelton, “I already won. I won you.” Shelton shoots back, “You’re not gonna reverse psychology me.”
