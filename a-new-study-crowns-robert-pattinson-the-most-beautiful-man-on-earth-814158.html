    Robert Pattinson is considered by many to be an exceptionally attractive man, with science backing up his appeal. Regarded as one of the closest to attaining “perfection,” the Twilight star, at 37 years old, possesses facial features and proportions that surpass even renowned heartthrobs like Brad Pitt and George Clooney.

    An amazing new technique was used to find the man with the “Golden Ratio.”

    SNAPPER/bauergriffinonline.com/East News, SNAPPER/bauergriffinonline.com/East News

    To discover the world’s most handsome man, renowned celebrity plastic surgeon Dr. Julian De Silva embraced science. Employing an innovative mapping technique, he compared facial features with the revered “Golden Ratio,” also known as the Greek beauty standard. This involved analyzing the facial proportions of popular Hollywood heartthrobs using carefully selected photographs.

    According to ancient Greek standards, Robert Pattinson is the “most beautiful man alive.”

    Hahn-Marechal-Nebinger/ABACA/EAST NEWS, Hahn-Marechal-Nebinger/ABACA/EAST NEWS

    Upon analyzing the findings, it was determined that Robert Pattinson possessed the most flawless facial features and details. Astonishingly, he achieved an impressive 92.15% match with the Greek concept of the “perfect man.” His eyes, nose, and chin were identified as his most outstanding attributes.

    Following closely behind Pattinson were esteemed individuals such as Henry Cavill, Bradley Cooper, Brad Pitt, and many others.

    Henry Cavill proved a formidable competitor, securing the second position as the world’s most handsome man with an impressive score of 91.64%. Bradley Cooper, known for his role in “A Star Is Born,” claimed the third spot, while Brad Pitt followed closely behind in fourth place. George Clooney rounded up the list, showcasing his enduring charm and securing a notable placement.

    The others that made the top ten were Hugh Jackman, David Beckham, Idris Elba, Kanye West, and Ryan Gosling. Ryan scored the best for his nose, and David Beckham received an almost perfect rating for his chin. Surprisingly, Jason Momoa didn’t make the cut.

    The same procedure was utilized to identify the world’s most beautiful woman.

    Applying the Golden Ratio, the esteemed supermodel Bella Hadid has been crowned the most beautiful woman in the world. Scoring an impressive 94.35% on the perfection scale, her exquisite chin was particularly highlighted. Joining her in the top 5 were renowned figures such as Beyoncé, Amber Heard, Ariana Grande, and Taylor Swift, all of whom radiated their unique charm and beauty.
