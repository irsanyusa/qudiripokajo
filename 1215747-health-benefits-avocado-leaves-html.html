    What is avocado?

    Avocado is a fruit in the form of a pear, ellipse, or a ball. While ripening, avocado increases in size and can reach 20 cm in length. The mass of ripe fruit varies from 50 g to 1.8 kg. If the fruit is not mature, then the skin that covers it will have a dark green color. The flesh of the fruit is fat, juicy, oily, and has a yellow-green or green color. It tastes slightly sweet, not sour.

    Avocado fruit contains many nutrients and contains no harmful fats and sugar at all. The composition of avocado includes oleic acid, potassium, sodium, calcium, phosphorus, manganese, magnesium, and other minerals, vitamins C, B, D, and E. This product is unique in its composition, and it has a beneficial effect on many organs of the human body and especially useful for beauty.

    Health benefits of avocado leaves

    Avocado leaves are rich in flavonol, quercetin, glycoside, polyphenol, estragole, alpha and beta-pinene, cineol, limonene, serotonin, flavonoids. All these nutrients that are very useful for our body. As we have said, they contain flavonol, which protects our body from degenerative diseases, such as osteoarthritis. Therefore, avocado leaves prevent the early signs of aging.
