    This week, Dancing with the Stars is celebrating Halloween with Monster Night, as the remaining nine couples perform spooky routines while impersonating scary creatures like vampires and zombies. The episode will also feature a Dance Marathon.

    What to Expect on DWTS Halloween Monster Night

    During Tuesday night’s show, Alyson Hannigan and her partner Sasha Farber will dance a Paso Doble to “Supermassive Black Hole” by Muse while dressed as vampires, while Xochitl Gomez and Val Chmerkovskiy will dance a Contemporary routine to “Game of Survival” by Ruelle while dressed as the “undead.”

    Ariana Madix and Pasha Pashkov will perform an Argentine Tango to “Bad Guy” by Billie Eilish while dressed as a harpy and harpy slayer, while Lele Pons and Brandon Armstrong will be a dead goth girl and a spider as they dance a Paso Doble to “Bloody Mary” (Wednesday dance TikTok version) by Lady Gaga.

    Charity Lawson and pro Artem Chigvintsev will be dressed as skeletons as they perform a Jive to “Skeleton Sam” by LVCRFT, while Jason Mraz and Danielle Karagach will be zombie/mummy hybrids in a Contemporary routine to “Zombie” by The Cranberries.

    Meanwhile, Harry Jowsey and Rylee Arnold will dance an Argentine Tango to “Blinding Lights” by District 78 while dressed as aliens. Barry Williams and Peta Murgatroyd will be a werewolf and Little Red Riding Hood as they dance a Viennese Waltz to “It’s A Man’s, Man’s, Man’s World” by James Brown.

    And finally, Mauricio Umansky will be a gored matador as he performs an Argentine Tango with his partner Emma Slater, set to “Somebody’s Watching Me” by Rockwell.

    There Will Also Be a Dance Marathon

    This week’s new episode of DWTS will also feature a Dance Marathon, or “Dance Monster-thon.” All nine couples will be on the floor at the same time, performing a Hustle to “Stayin’ Alive” by the Bee Gees and a Charleston to “Grim Grinning Ghosts (Dance Party)” by Kris Bowers.

    The judges will tap couples out one at a time. Contestants will be able to earn bonus points based on how long they stay in the marathon. These points will be added to their combined scores for their individual dances.

    Tuesday’s episode will also feature Niecy Nash as a guest judge. You can watch it all go down Tuesday night at 8/7c on ABC or Disney+.
